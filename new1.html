Ridge Park & Flower Exhibition Center

The Ridge is a flat wonderful stretch of road just above the Gangtok Town. It would be about 20 minutes walk from MG Marg Main Market. The Ridge is flanked on one side by the White Hall as well as the Chief Minister's Residence, and on the other side by a wonderful gate with pagoda like top. 

Tashi View Point

 It offers a sweeping view of Kanchenjunga snow peaks on a clear day. In fact the view of sunrise from here is unforgettable, you will see the peaks dazzling with changing colors.

You have to climb some steps for the view though

MG Marg Main Market

MG Marg (Road) is one of the main shopping and cultural activity streets in Gangtok. The road and the area around it has transformed over the years. It is now more like a long stretch of open mall where locals and tourists just sit and relax on one of the many benches laid along the road to soak in the ambiance. This place is a 'must visit' because without it a visit to Gangtok is really incomplete.
